A Journey to Cafayate

Discover on of Argentina's gems: Cafayate. A dreamy destination only 3 hours drive from Salta (Capital).
Step out into a world from another planet, with scenery that makes you feel like you were in Mars.

Left: Castillos de Cafayate. Right: La Yesera.

A place that will captivate your heart. There is a multitude of things to do in Cafayate.
There are dozens of free hikes to do from about 30 minutes drive from Cafayate, which are not full of tourists. There are over 25 wineries to enjoy high-altitude wines. You can indulge in some of the best empanadas in Argentina. If you need restaurant, wineries, hosting or activities recommendations, please contact me. I'd be happy to share my experience!

The food, the wineries, the landscapes, the architecture... nothing short of breathtaking.

The drive from Salta to Cafayate is beautiful: you go from a lush sub-tropical jungle to desert.
